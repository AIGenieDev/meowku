# Privacy Policy for Meowku: Cat Sudoku Puzzle

**Last updated:** July 23, 2026

**SiliconGenie** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Meowku: Cat Sudoku Puzzle** mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Meowku: Cat Sudoku Puzzle does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. All game progress, high scores, and settings are saved locally on your device.

## 2. In-App Purchases & Google Play Services
Our application uses Google Play Billing Services to handle optional in-app purchases (such as the Ultra plan). We do not store or process your financial or payment details on our servers. All purchase transactions are processed securely by Google Play.
For more details, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. Data Retention and Security
Local game progress remains on your device until you uninstall the application or clear its storage data.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our game is designed to be family-friendly and safe for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
